Big Band

The Super Super big band is an LGBT big band founded in 2022. We play a range of styles from early New Orleans to blues to latin to modern hits. Our goals are to bring excellect performances to the public, create learning and development opportunities for musicians, and increase visibility of the LGBT community.

Jams

The Super Super hosts a monthly jam session in a friendly and welcoming atmosphere for all levels. Our goal is to learn tunes written by womyn composers and create a space free from discouraging competition and gatekeeping. Contact us for jam session details.

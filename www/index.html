The Super Super

San Francisco's world famous LGBT+ Big Band

The Super Super is an LGBT+ big band founded in 2022. We play a range of styles from early New Orleans to blues to latin to modern hits. Our goals are to bring excellect performances to the public, create learning and development opportunities for musicians, and increase visibility of the LGBT+ community.

Jams

The Super Super is proud to unite with local non-profit Bay Puddles Arts to host a monthly jam session for women and FLINTA persons in a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Don't let fear keep you from playing! Our goal is to make space for all players in an encouraging atmosphere, as well as to learn standards composed by womyn. Instructors participate in the jams to help guide you.

Contact us for jam session details.

Play with Us

We always want to meet new players. Even when our performance roster is full we'd like to invite new players to sub at rehearsals. Our jam sessions are intended to preserve space for LGBT+ players and womyn but are open to all. Please contact us for jam session details.
